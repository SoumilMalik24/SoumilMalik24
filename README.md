## ABOUT ME

I am a **Computer Science undergraduate** focused on **applied Machine Learning, Generative AI, and MLOps engineering**.

Rather than stopping at model accuracy, I focus on **end-to-end ML systems** —  
from **data ingestion and experimentation** to **deployment, monitoring, and reproducibility**.

My work emphasizes:
- Strong ML & Deep Learning fundamentals
- Production-oriented system design
- Clean, traceable, and reproducible pipelines
- Engineering rigor over isolated models
